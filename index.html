About Us

Operating since 2016, Wentworth Computer Science Society is Wentworth Institute of Technology's premier computer science student organization. Hosting weekly workshops, networking events, and guest lectures, we aim to provide an inclusive and welcoming community where students can gain experience and education, as well as network professionally. We are also responsible for the organization of wentworth's biggest student run event, HackWITus.
